# Credit_Card_Financial_Dashboard
End-to-End ETL Pipeline: Credit Card Transactions &amp; Customer Insights Dashboard

Credit Card Financial Weekly Dashboard

Project Objective:
Developed a comprehensive Power BI dashboard to provide real-time insights into key performance metrics and credit card operation trends, enabling stakeholders to monitor and analyze business performance effectively.

Technical Workflow:
Data Management (SQL): Developed and managed a SQL database to store transaction and customer data. This involved preparing CSV data, creating relational tables, and importing over 10,000 rows of data into the SQL environment for analysis.Data Processing & DAX: Streamlined data processing by implementing complex DAX queries to create custom metrics and groupings.Customer Segmentation: Created age (e.g., 20-30, 30-40) and income groups (Low, Med, High) for deeper demographic analysis.

Financial Metrics: Calculated key measures including total Revenue (sum of annual fees, transaction amounts, and interest earned) and Week-on-Week (WoW) revenue growth.

Dashboard Development: Built an interactive dashboard in Power BI to visualize performance trends and monitor key metrics in real-time.

Key Insights & Impact:Revenue Performance: Analyzed a total Year-to-Date (YTD) revenue of $57M, with $8M generated from interest and $46M from total transaction volume.

Growth Trends: Identified a 28.8% increase in revenue for the final week of the year (Week 53).

Customer Demographics: Discovered that male customers contributed $31M to revenue compared to $26M from female customers.

Market Concentration: Identified that Blue and Silver credit cards account for 93% of overall transactions, and 68% of revenue is concentrated in three states: TX, NY, and CA.
Operational Metrics: Monitored critical health indicators, including an overall Activation rate of 57.5% and a Delinquent rate of 6.06%.

Actionable Outcomes:
Provided stakeholders with data-driven insights to support strategic decision-making processes and shared findings to improve operational efficiency.
